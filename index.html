About me

Hi, I'm Tristan. I'm a young data analyst and I would love to spend passionate hours, investigating, coding and manipulating data to uncover mysteries and help you make the right decisions, a data-driven decision.

I learned data science during my engineering school (France) but opportunities led me to another path, the one of product engineer/manager. After my MSc Engineering and some interesting work experiences in this field, I did an MSc Marketing (UK) to better understand the business context and to be more customer oriented.

There, my favorite course was the one about data: Market Research Methods, and my closest friends were studying data science. All this got me thinking. I therefore changed my plan to follow what felt like a passion for me: data science.

I already had some skills (statistics, R, SQL) thanks to my engineering school but I decided to spend some months on DataCamp to improve my skills on R and SQL and learn Python and Tableau.

I'm now fully ready to work as a data analyst and can't wait to code and investigate on your data.

Ps: I love challenges, I love learning new things, and I love working with others.
