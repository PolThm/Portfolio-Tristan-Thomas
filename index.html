About me

Hi, I'm Tristan. A young data analyst who would love to spend passionate hours, investigating, coding and manipulating data to uncover mysteries and help you make the right decisions.

If you look at my CV (in the menu), you will see that data has always been part of my background, but also that opportunities led me to work as a product engineer/manager during my MSc Engineering (France). Those experiences are a strength for me. They have given me complementary skills that other data analysts do not have.

Indeed, they allowed me to develop my interpersonal and project management skills but also my analytical thinking and problem-solving approach. In addition to that, I also did an MSc Marketing (UK) to better understand the business context and be more customer-oriented.

Finally, after reinforcing my data skills from my MSc Engineering with Datacamp in 2021 (SQL, R, Python, Tableau, Excel, Statistics), I am now fully ready to work as a data analyst and cannot wait to investigate on your data.

Ps: I love challenges, I love learning new things, and I love working with others.
