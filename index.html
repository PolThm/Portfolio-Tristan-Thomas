About me

Hi, I'm Tristan. A young data analyst who would love to spend passionate hours investigating, coding and manipulating data to uncover mysteries and help you make the right decisions.

If you look at my CV, you will see that data has always been part of my career, but also that opportunities have led me to work as an engineer / product manager during my MSc Engineering (France). These experiences gave me complementary skills, I think they are a strength for me now.

Indeed, they allowed me to develop my relational and project management skills but also my analytical mind and my problem-solving approach. In addition to that, I also did an MSc Marketing (UK) to better understand the business context and be more customer-oriented.

Finally, after strengthening my data skills from my MSc Engineering with data-camp in 2021 (SQL, R, Python, Tableau, Excel, Statistics), I am now fully ready to work as a data analyst and have looking forward to investigating your data.

Ps: I love challenges, I love learning new things, and I love working with others.
